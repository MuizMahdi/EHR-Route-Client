EHRoute

A secure, and free Electronic Health Records (EHR) storage, management, and sharing solution.

This desktop application serves as a proof-of-concept for the proposed semi-centralized architecure. This project proves the feasibility of a software architecture where clients store their data locally and share it through a central server that works as a message broker between the clients. Consisting of networks, where patients' data transfer are governed by themselves, and where healthcare providers are able to access and manage the EHRs of their networks.
